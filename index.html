About Me

I am currently pursuing my Master of Science in Computer Science (MSCS) at the University of Southern California (USC) in Los Angeles.


My academic journey began at the Universitat Politècnica de València (UPV), where I developed a strong foundation in computer engineering. In 2024, I was honored to be selected as a Fulbright Scholar, a distinction that supported my transition to the U.S. to further my research in Artificial Intelligence.


Beyond the lab and lecture hall, I am a competitive athlete. I play FIBA 3x3 Basketball, having competed nationally in Spain. This experience has taught me the value of quick decision-making, teamwork, and resilience—skills I apply daily to my engineering challenges.
